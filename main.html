Rujia Li

Rujia Li (李佳轩) is a CS PhD candidate at University of Birmingham and Southern University of Science and Technology (Joint PhD Program). He is advised by Dr.David Galindo , Dr.Qi Wang and Prof. Mark Ryan . His principal research interests lie in the field of blockchain security analysis, defining and constructing cryptographic protocols based on blockchain and formal security proofs. He is a member of the Birmingham Centre for Cyber Security and Privacy.

Research Interests:

  • Formal security proofs.
  • Blockchain security analysis.
  • Privacy-preserving smart contracts*
  • Cryptographic protocols based on blockchain.
