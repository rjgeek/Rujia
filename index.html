Research Interests

His research focuses on Privacy-Preserving Decentralized Systems (PPDSs), with particular emphasis on improving the (economic-level and system-level) security of hybrid consensus algorithms and hybrid privacy-preserving technologies that underpin such systems.

Research

Rujia‚Äôs research on Privacy-Preserving Decentralized Systems (PPDSs) is driven by a simple conviction: INDIVIDUALS SHOULD RETAIN MEANINGFUL CONTROL OVER THEIR PERSONAL DATA. He views privacy and data autonomy not as obstacles, but as enabling conditions for trustworthy large-scale data utilization. Guided by this motivation, his work on PPDSs aims to support data-driven collaboration with strong guarantees of privacy, accountability, and user control, without relying on centralized trust.
